About

Hi, I'm Izzy.

I'm a San Francisco-based traveler and tech professional. I've spent significant time in China over the past decade — living there, working there, and getting hopelessly lost in its night markets — and I keep being amazed by how fast the country is moving.

China Tech Tour started because friends in tech kept asking the same questions. Is WeChat really everything? How does Shenzhen actually feel? Where do I start? The best way I knew to answer was to take them.

The tours are built for curious American technologists in their late twenties and thirties — people who read Stratechery, follow founders on Twitter, and want a real, unfiltered look at the country they keep reading about. We visit hardware factories and AI labs, sit down with operators and founders, eat extraordinarily well, and leave room for the unplanned conversations that always end up being the most memorable part of the trip.

If any of that resonates, I'd love to hear from you. Reach out, or follow along on Instagram for dispatches from the road.
